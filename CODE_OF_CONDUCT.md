# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment. By participating, you agree to abide by these rules:

- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Assume good intent; seek to understand before challenging.
- Harassment and discrimination are not tolerated.
- Keep content professional; no NSFW content.
- Respect privacy; do not share sensitive information.

Enforcement may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans. Report issues to maintainers via GitHub issues or email if available.
